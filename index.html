About Us

Rising Point Academy, located in Malviya Nagar, South Delhi, is a premier coaching institute dedicated to excellence in Science and Mathematics education. Founded by Mr. Hari Prakash Shrivastav, a distinguished postgraduate in Mathematics from DDU Gorakhpur University, our institute represents the culmination of academic excellence and innovative teaching methodologies.

Mr. Shrivastav, after a successful stint in the industry, followed his passion for teaching and established Rising Point Academy. His vision of creating an institute that not only imparts knowledge but also builds character has been the driving force behind our success.
